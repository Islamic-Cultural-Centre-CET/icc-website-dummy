Islamic Cultural Centre

ICC was founded in 1989 by a group of well guided muslims of College of Engineering, Trivandrum. It has now evolved into an organisation which caters the needs of muslims in the college. ICC had its humble beginnings in a rented apartment and now it has flourished into a well structured organisation occupying a three storey building.

About Us

Islamic Cultural Centre is an organisation established to identify, assist and aid the muslim community of College of Engineering Trivandrum. It is located at the close proximity opposite to the college. ICC has been running under the leadership of Prof. Abdul Rasheed, Retired HOD of Mechanical Department CET, from its inception in 1989 onwards.
Every year a new body of ICC Committee is elected and carry out the plans and activities of the year including weekly Qur'an classes, SQUIF activities, Islamic quiz, Islamic classes by prominent scholars and so on. ICC also provides mental and financial support to the students in need. ICC also has Student Mess and Library that provides books of syllabus related publications of various departments and also contains islamic books and Hadith's. ICC has grown over the past years with overwhelming number of students registering every year and with a huge network of Alumnis.
ICC has evolved throughout the year from a rented apartment to a bigger three storey building having various facilities including student mess, library, praying hall. Annual elections were held to select new committee to lead the community towards more progressive goals. Hostel facilities were made nearby for fresher students. ICC has become the intersection where students of different backgrounds interacts irrespective of political affiliations and other islamic organisations
